About me

Hi! My name is Zsolt. I'm a self-taught web developer. I am absolutely dedicated by programming. I am a kind of beginner but I am enjoying learning new skills, which I see as a key component to my future success. I started my journey on the Codecademy website. At the beginning it was only a hobby and it is remained in the same position for a couple of years as I did not have time to learn next to my job. Later I realized I want to do something more in this industry as I have fallen in with Web Development, so I joined FreeCodeCamp as well. I've already learned so much and am excited for what lies ahead.

I made this website using Bootstrap as it is really responsive and simple, Font-Awesome and I added in Google Fonts to present some nice fonts.

I was encouraged to use the Read-Search-Ask method from the freeCodeCamp and managed to put all of this together just using Read and Search! I found websites such as Stackoverflow invaluable. Most of the questions I had was already answered.

I am learning JavaScript, NodeJS, Express, Mongo database at the moment by Udemy and FreeCodeCamp. My target is to become a Full Stack Web Developer or more but no less!
